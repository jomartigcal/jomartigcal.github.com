Android Applications

Personal Android (and Android Wear) applications developed by Jomar Tigcal have been published on Google Play Store.

Sweldong Pinoy
Sweldong Pinoy is an Android app for Filipinos that allows them to compute their gross and net pays, withholding taxes, and contributions to SSS/GSIS, Philhealth and PAG-IBIG.
Pinoy Jokes
Pinoy Jokes is an Android app that will allow users to view and share Filipino Jokes.
GDG Watch Face
GDG Watch Face is an Android Wear Watch Face which features an analog clock and a digital display. You can customize the watch face by showing/hiding the date and by changing the colors of the hands (hour, minute, second) and hour markers.
Help Me
Help Me is an Android and Android Wear emergency button app that allows you to send an SMS to your specified contact person with a click of a button. It won second place in Accenture Technology Talks & Hackathon 2015.
GDG Philippines
Get the latest posts, events, and other updates about GDG Philippines using the official Android app.
Boto Ko
Boto Ko is an Android app where you can use to see the list of Filipino candidates and select the ones you want to vote for
Thirteenth Month
Thirteenth Month is an Android app and web app for Filipinos in computing their 13th-month pays
PHostpaid
PHostpaid is a utility Android app for postpaid mobile phone services in the Philippines.
Budget Pinoy
Budget Pinoy is a tool for Filipinos in computing gross pay, net pay, and asking rate based on user's budget..
Tigcal Utilities
Tigcal Utilities is a collection of utility mini-apps. This app includes the following utilities: Battery, Bill Calculator, and Flashlight.

Google Assistant Apps

Applications for Google Assistant developed by Jomar Tigcal

Budget Penny
Budget Penny is your budget assistant that's ready to help in managing your finances. Simply tell her your monthly budget and update her of your expenses as often as you like. Moreover, you can ask her how much is your remaining balance or whether you still have budget.
GDG Philippines
GDG Philippines: Official Google Assistant App for the Google Developers Group (GDG) Philippines community. You can ask it about the upcoming events and more information about GDG Philippines.
